Course Description

This course introduces the fundamentals of air transportation systems, including basics of aviation, aircraft types, airport operations, air traffic management and aviation safety. It explains how the air transportation industry functions, including regulatory bodies and future developments in aerospace.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the structure of air transportation system
  • Learn fundamentals of passenger and cargo transport
  • Study civil aviation regulatory policies
  • Understand airport operations
  • Discuss safety standards and future trends
