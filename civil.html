Course Description

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of structural engineering, including basics of structural analysis, material behavior, design principles, loading conditions, safety standards, and modern construction practices. Students will learn the engineering aspects of designing safe and efficient structures.

Course Objectives

  • Understand fundamental structural analysis concepts
  • Learn about structural materials and their behavior
  • Study design methods and safety codes
  • Analyze loading conditions on structures
  • Explore modern structural engineering techniques
